The Modern History Of Electronic Hardcore
2011
During 2011 Artists like Angerfist started to gain popularity quickly. The hardcore scene was thriving with many new producers and labels making their mark on the scene not only in Europe but in the rest of the world as well. A hardcore DJ even appeared at North America's biggest music festival. In 2011 Angerfist entered the DJ Mag Top 100 at position #39
2015
Genres like Frenchcore, Uptempo, Hardcore and Terrorcore became more popular around this time. The average BPM of a hardcore song also shifted from 160-180 BPM to 200+ BPM.
2019
Hardcore is just as popular as ever and 2019s edition of Thunderdome reached an attendance of almost 40,000 people and has become the largest hardcore event to ever take place. Regular large scale events started happening outside of the Netherlands and began popping up in other European Countries. Though sadly in America Hardcore still counts as a relatively underground genre
