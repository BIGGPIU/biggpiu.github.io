DJ MYOSUKE
A HARDCORE DJ/Producer who specializes in high-speed, distorted tracks. He launched and is in charge of direction of the sub-label "HARDCORE TANO*C HARDCORE". He continues to be active at the forefront of the scene both in Japan and overseas, and is a participant of "Dogfight Records" led by DJ Mad Dog. In addition to working on music for many music games such as WACCA, Chunithm, Taiko no Tatsujin, and Arcaea, he is also active in many other areas, such as running multiple labels and club events.

Kobaryo
He started his musical career in 2010. He mainly produces speedcore and mainstream hardcore. Many of his songs use emotional melodies, but his style is wide, sometimes with ethnic or inorganic sounds. He also uses multiple names and produces songs in various genres such as EDM, trance, hardstyle, and UK hardcore, making the most of his natural dexterity. In speedcore, he specializes in versatile sounds that draw on a large amount of sampling and synths in various directions, and songs with speedy and chaotic developments. In recent years, he has also participated in overseas hardcore compilation CDs, expanding his activities overseas.

Laur
In 2015, he started composing music under the name Laur. At the age of 19, he joined the Japanese speedcore label Psycho Filth Records and participated in many albums. He has also been providing music for KONAMI's music game SOUND VOLTEX, as well as the social music game Arcaea, which has been a hot topic. He also started DJing in 2017, and has established a grand and unique style that excels in fusing club music with orchestral music, ethnic music, and other genres. He is a young composer who is attracting attention for his continued exploration of music that is not bound by conventions. He released his first solo album The Angel's Message in April 2018.
